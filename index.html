Journal of Computer Science and Technology

Welcome to the official website of the Journal of Computer Science and Technology (JCST). We aim to publish high-quality research papers in all areas of computer science, including artificial intelligence, software engineering, data science, and more.

About Our Journal

JCST is a peer-reviewed open-access journal that provides a platform for researchers and practitioners to share their innovative research findings and technical contributions. Our editorial board consists of leading experts from around the world to ensure the quality and impact of published papers.

Key Information

  • Peer Review Process: 2-4 weeks
  • Publication Frequency: Monthly
  • Open Access: Free for readers, low publication fee for authors
